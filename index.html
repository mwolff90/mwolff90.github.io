About Me

Hi,
my name is Markus Wolff. I'm a professional Programmer/ Data Scientist & DevOps engineer from Germany.
I've started programming in C++ during middle school about 1 year before I started my IT apprenticeship @Genossenschaftsverband_Bayern from 2007 to 2009.
During which I discovered a drive to analyse data, at that time just using Java to wrangle data dumps from the databases.
During and after finishing my B.Sc. in Computer Science @University_of_Applied:Sciences_Rosenheim, I've been able to apply my knowledge @ Deloitte Germany by supporting the Risk Advisory team until 2017.

In hindsight, leaving the great team there was surely my biggest mistake careerwise, but I moved on to @University_of_Salzburg learn more about Data Science. The mainly mathematical and theoretical appoach to real world problems just didn't really fit with me, so I moved on to foreign waters.
I wanted to apply myself in ways, I've never before thought about, by supporting @Schoen_Klinik_SE as DevOps-Engineer and Technical Application Manager.
